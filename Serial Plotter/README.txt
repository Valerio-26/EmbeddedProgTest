Serial Plotter to read analog and digital values from serial terminal. To make sounds with analog values click on Sound toggle(Adjust frequency map to adapt it to your analog values). [Best sounds with headphones]

You can send commands only in Wait Request and Pause mode.
Commands:
raw (default mode) - to remove any filter from the analog signal.
moving average - apply moving average filter with 150 elements to the analog signal.
random noise - apply/remove random noise to/from output analog signal. (Work in both raw and moving average mode).
